1. To play the game, press the button and insert your name.Name will affect ranking systems.

4. Letters will be marked as green or red depending on whether the answer you guessed is right or wrong.For wrong answers you will be given the correct one.

5. If you don't know the word, you can save it for later by typing the word 'PASAPALABRA'.
